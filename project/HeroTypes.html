Hero Types

There are several heroes within Dota2. However; there are 2 role types that all heroes are confined too. These hero roles are Support or Core(carry/dps). The roles can vary from position 1 to 5, depending on how much support or carry they provide to the team. Each Hero has Agility , Intelligence, and Strength. However; heroes only have 1 main attribute that they benefit the most from that defines whether they are agility, int, or strength. There are 117 heroes in dota 2, with around 2 new heroes that are released and added to the game.
