Overview

Dota 2 is a very popular moba. A moba is a genre of a game such as rpg or mmorpg is a genre. MOBA based games however; are usually extremely competitive since the players that play these games want to be recognized as great players usually even the best. Dota itself has 5 players in each team with 2 teams pitted against each other. The entire point of the game is to destroy the enemies ancient before they have a chance to destroy yours. However, its not as simple as that. There are over a hundred heroes for a player to choose and play as with hundreds of different items. Each player can choose up to 6 items that from the hundreds. The amount of choices and options within this game alongside consistent major updates that happen yearly have kept this game alive for over 15 years.
